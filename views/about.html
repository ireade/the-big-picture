About

The Big Picture is an app for keeping track of your long-term goals, and the small, acheivable tasks you need to complete them.

Privacy

The data from this is stored securely in an external database. This is because I am hoping to make the app available on other platforms (e.g. Android) in the future so it can't be stored in the browser.
